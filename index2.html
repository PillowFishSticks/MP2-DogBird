Dogbird

Game

Is it a dog? Is it a bird? No, its Dogbird! A game of skill, luck, and chance. You need to dodge pipes, catch treats and fly like your life depends on it. Best of luck!

The aim of the game is to keep flying for as long as possible. You need to dodge the pipes while maneuvering between them. While dodging pipes, you need to try and catch the bones, which when caught increase your score. Be careful though, the longer you player, the harder it gets!

Click the start button to begin the game. Use your mouse or touch pad to move up and dodge obstacles. Gravity will bring you back down to earth.
